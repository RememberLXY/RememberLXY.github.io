## 个人简介

我是北京大学公共卫生学院卫生政策与管理专业的硕士研究生，同时拥有该学院预防医学专业的医学学士学位。我的学术与研究兴趣集中于健康信息化、卫生政策分析、公共卫生数据科学，以及数字工具在卫生体系改善中的应用。

I am a master's student in Health Policy and Management at the [School of Public Health](https://sph.pku.edu.cn/), [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/). I am also a graduate with a Bachelor of Medicine in Preventive Medicine from the same institution. My academic and research interests focus on health informatics, health policy analysis, public health data science, and the application of digital tools in health system improvement.
